Core Capabilities (LTX Context)

Property ManagementManage land parcels, ownership links, attributes, subdivisions and consolidations used to determine land tax liability.
Ownership ManagementMaintain accurate ownership relationships across individuals, companies, trusts and joint owners for land tax purposes.
Valuation ManagementManage valuation data supplied by the Valuer-General and apply it correctly in land tax assessments.
Liability ManagementCalculate, adjust and manage land tax liabilities including interest, penalties and write-offs.
Assessment ManagementGenerate, issue, amend and store land tax assessments and notices.
Exemption ManagementApply, assess, approve, revoke and manage land tax exemptions and concessions.
Payment ManagementReceive, allocate, reconcile and manage land tax payments and instalments.
Refund ManagementProcess refunds arising from overpayments or reassessments.
Adjustment ManagementApply financial or non-financial adjustments resulting from changed circumstances.
Clearance Certificate ManagementIssue and manage clearance certificates confirming land tax status.
