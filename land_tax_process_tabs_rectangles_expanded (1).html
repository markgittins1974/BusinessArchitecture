Administer Land Ownership & Notice of Acquisition (LOAN)

This process manages how changes in land ownership are validated and applied following receipt of a Notice of Acquisition (NOA). The process is decision-led and exception-aware, ensuring ownership, property, party and exemption information is only updated when authoritative and legally defensible.
