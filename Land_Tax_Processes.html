Administer Land Ownership & Notice of Acquisition (LOAN)

This process manages how changes in land ownership are validated and applied following receipt of a Notice of Acquisition (NOA). It is decision‑led, ensuring property, party, ownership and exemption data are updated only when authoritative and legally defensible.
