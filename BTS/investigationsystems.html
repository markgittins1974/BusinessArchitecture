Systems mapped to Investigation Lifecycle (Current State)

1. Identify Issues & Triggers

  • Intelligence & Referral Management
    • COI – intelligence, referrals, trigger lists
    • Microsoft Outlook – referral and coordination emails
  • Risk Indicators & Analytics
    • COI – risk indicators and analytics

2. Triage & Initiate Case

  • Investigation Intake & Triage
    • COI – review trigger and intelligence context
    • e‑Sys – create investigation case (often post‑triage)
    • Microsoft Excel – prioritisation and working notes
    • Microsoft Outlook – internal discussion
  • Jurisdiction & Authority Validation
    • ASIC – company and director checks
    • ABN Lookup / ABR – entity validation
    • VEDA / Equifax – identity and insolvency checks
    • e‑Sys – confirm entity and case linkage
  • Investigation Case Management
    • e‑Sys – primary system of record
    • Microsoft Excel – shadow workload and stage tracking
  • Automated & Bulk Investigation Initiation
    • Not supported – manual case creation in e‑Sys
  • Investigation Activity & Effort Tracking
    • Microsoft Excel – primary activity tracking
    • e‑Sys – status updates (often lagging)

3. Plan & Gather Evidence

  • Investigation Planning
    • Microsoft Excel – plans, sequencing, timelines
    • Microsoft Word – planning notes
    • e‑Sys – high‑level case context
  • Information Requests
    • Microsoft Outlook – issue and manage requests
    • Microsoft Word – letters and notices
    • Phone – clarifications and follow‑ups
  • Evidence & Information Capture
    • CM10 – store evidence and correspondence
    • Microsoft Outlook – source of inbound evidence
    • e‑Sys – references to evidence
  • External & Internal Search Management
    • ASIC
    • ABN Lookup / ABR
    • VEDA / Equifax
    • COI – supplementary intelligence
  • Investigation Flags & Non‑Issue Controls
    • e‑Sys – closure and non‑progression recorded
    • Microsoft Excel / Outlook – informal rationale capture

4. Assess & Decide

  • Investigation Information Management
    • CM10 – authoritative evidence store
    • e‑Sys – case summaries and outcomes
  • Evidence Analysis & Decision Support
    • Microsoft Excel – calculations and comparisons
    • Microsoft Word – analysis and draft decisions
    • e‑Sys – final decision recording
  • Legal & Policy Review
    • Microsoft Word – policy interpretation
    • Microsoft Outlook – peer and manager consultation
  • Investigation Period & Revenue Line Management
    • e‑Sys – revenue line and period fields
    • Microsoft Excel – working views and checks

5. Enforce & Govern Outcomes

  • Sanctions & Remedies Management
    • Microsoft Word – outcome documentation
    • e‑Sys – sanctions recorded
    • Microsoft Outlook – outcome communication
  • Internal Approvals
    • Microsoft Outlook – approval via email
  • Governed Exception & Override Management
    • Microsoft Outlook – exception discussion
    • e‑Sys – retrospective recording
  • Investigation Reporting & Performance Insights
    • Microsoft Word – investigation reports
    • Microsoft Excel – performance tracking
    • e‑Sys – limited reporting
    • COI – aggregate intelligence reporting
