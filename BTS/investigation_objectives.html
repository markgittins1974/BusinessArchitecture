Case Management Transformation – Alignment Views

This artefact defines a shared success narrative and role‑alignment view. It is not a benefits realisation, assurance, or delivery tracking artefact.

Executive View
Management View
Product View

⚡ Faster resolution without increased risk

  • Investigations completed materially faster end‑to‑end
  • Low‑value or simple cases resolved early
  • Effort shifts to judgement and analysis
Related operational pain points
  • "Half of PICs shouldn’t be investigations and still proceed too far"
  • "Low‑value cases progress deep into investigation due to lack of early decision criteria"
  • "Investigation planning often starts without a clear plan, causing delay and rework"
  • "Case closure takes longer than the investigation itself due to administrative steps"

🛡️ Consistent and defensible outcomes

  • Standard frameworks and evidence use
  • Reduced variation in decisions
  • Fewer overturned cases
Related operational pain points
  • "Risk assessment is subjective and inconsistent, with no consistent risk framework across teams"
  • "Evidence interpretation varies by investigator"
  • "Templates and notices are inconsistent, increasing rework and objection effort"
  • "Objections often require full re‑analysis, indicating defensibility gaps"

👁️ Clear line of sight and accountability

  • Traceable decision paths
  • Clear ownership and approvals
  • Lower audit burden
Related operational pain points
  • "PICs come from multiple sources with no consistent intake pathway"
  • "Lack of clear intake criteria leads to misrouted work and cases that shouldn’t proceed"
  • "Approvals are manual and email‑driven, which is slow and not auditable"
  • "No single source of truth for grouping or end‑to‑end status visibility"

♻️ Learning system

  • Consistent capture of outcomes
  • Pattern‑based insights
  • Improving future decisions
Related operational pain points
  • "Learnings are not captured systematically"
  • "Knowledge remains in individuals’ heads rather than reusable guidance"
  • "No structured mechanism to share insights or patterns across teams"
  • "Insights do not flow back into risk models or upstream teams"

🤖 AI‑enabled success

  • AI supports, not replaces, judgement
  • Improved synthesis and insight
  • Governed AI maturity
Related operational pain points
  • "Investigators spend excessive time manually searching and “Googling”"
  • "Large unstructured documents are difficult to analyse consistently"
  • "Payroll data arrives in many different formats"
  • "Evidence and documents are hard to find across CM9 and other repositories"

⚡ Managing resolution

  • Monitoring case ageing and bottlenecks
  • Assessing early closures
  • Identifying rework drivers

🛡️ Managing consistency

  • Detecting decision outliers
  • Ensuring framework adherence
  • Reviewing outcome alignment

👁️ Managing accountability

  • Tracking approval turnaround
  • Managing scope creep
  • Reducing handovers

♻️ Managing learning

  • Assessing insight quality
  • Identifying systemic issues
  • Feeding guidance and training

🤖 Managing AI‑supported decision‑making

  • Monitoring AI usage boundaries
  • Maintaining human accountability
  • Detecting drift or bias
  • Ensuring policy‑aligned evolution
