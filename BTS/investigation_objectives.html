Case Management Transformation – Alignment Views

This artefact defines a shared success narrative and role‑alignment view. It is not intended to serve as a benefits realisation, assurance, or delivery tracking artefact.

Executive View
Management View
Product View (HMWs)

⚡ Faster resolution without increased risk

  • Investigations completed materially faster end‑to‑end
  • Low‑value or simple cases resolved early
  • Effort shifts to judgement and analysis

🛡️ Consistent and defensible outcomes

  • Standard frameworks and evidence use
  • Reduced variation in decisions
  • Fewer overturned cases

👁️ Clear line of sight and accountability

  • Traceable decision paths
  • Clear ownership and approvals
  • Lower audit burden

♻️ Learning system

  • Consistent capture of outcomes
  • Pattern‑based insights
  • Improving future decisions

🤖 AI‑enabled success

  • AI supports, not replaces, judgement
  • Improved synthesis and insight
  • Governed AI maturity

⚡ Managing resolution

  • Monitoring case ageing and bottlenecks by stage
  • Assessing volume and rationale for early closures
  • Identifying recurring rework drivers

🛡️ Managing consistency

  • Detecting outliers in risk scoring and decisions
  • Ensuring consistent use of assessment frameworks
  • Reviewing preliminary vs final outcome alignment

👁️ Managing accountability

  • Tracking approval turnaround times
  • Monitoring scope and plan changes
  • Reducing excessive handovers

♻️ Managing learning

  • Assessing quality of learning capture
  • Identifying recurring issues and patterns
  • Feeding insights into guidance and training

🤖 Managing AI‑supported decision‑making

  • Monitoring when AI‑assisted insight is used versus bypassed
  • Ensuring human accountability remains clear in AI‑supported judgements
  • Watching for drift, bias, or over‑reliance in AI‑assisted decisions
  • Ensuring AI use evolves in line with trust, policy, and governance expectations
