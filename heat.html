Capability and process maturity views across all six value stages — including Common capabilities.

▶ What am I looking at?

Capability Heat Map shows every L3 capability placed in its value stage column. Each card shows a maturity badge and any related procedures as pills.

Process Heat Map groups capabilities under the process that executes them. Work items are shown with name, KPI days, and items on hand where linked.

Maturity represents documentation completeness only:
HIGH — Process and procedures documented.
MEDIUM — Process mapped but no procedures, or procedures exist but no process mapped.
LOW — No process or procedures documented.
