📚 What Do These Metrics Mean?
VOLUME
Simple Definition: How many times this process runs. Higher volume = bigger financial impact.

Example: If Volume = 1,000, the process runs 1,000 times. If it costs $50 per run, total cost is $50,000.
COST PER ITEM
Simple Definition: How much it costs to run the process one time, including labor.

How It's Calculated: Time Standard (minutes) × Cost per Minute
Example: If a process takes 15 minutes and the worker costs $4/min, then Cost per Item = $60
RTY (Right The First Time) - 0 to 100%
Simple Definition: What percentage of the time the process works perfectly without needing to be redone.

What it means:
  • 100% RTY = Perfect. Zero errors, zero rework.
  • 80% RTY = 80% correct first time, 20% need redoing = wasted money
  • 50% RTY = Half the work gets done right, half gets redone = massive waste
Business Impact: Rework is pure waste. Every 1% improvement in RTY saves money.
PCE (Process Capability) - 0 to 100%
Simple Definition: How much of the process is consistent and within acceptable limits.

What it means:
  • 100% PCE = Process is perfectly controlled and stable
  • 80% PCE = Most of the time it works well, sometimes it drifts
  • 30% PCE = Process is unpredictable and inconsistent
Business Impact: Low PCE creates variability, customer complaints, and unexpected costs.
EFFICIENCY SCORE - 0 to 100%
Simple Definition: Overall process health combining quality (RTY) and consistency (PCE).

Calculation: (RTY + PCE) ÷ 2

What it means:
  • 75%+ Efficiency = Excellent - Process is optimized
  • 55-75% Efficiency = Good - Process works but needs tuning
  • 35-55% Efficiency = At Risk - Improvement needed
  • <35% Efficiency = Critical - Urgent action required
TOTAL COST
Simple Definition: Total cost to run the process for all items/transactions.

Calculation: Volume × Cost per Item

Example: 1,000 items × $50 per item = $50,000 total cost
AVOIDABLE COST
Simple Definition: Money wasted due to errors and rework that could be saved.

Calculation: Total Cost × (1 - RTY)

Example: If Total Cost = $50,000 and RTY = 80% (20% rework)
Avoidable Cost = $50,000 × 0.20 = $10,000 in waste

This is your biggest opportunity for savings.
