Critical Risk Assessment

🔴 CRITICAL: Executive Succession Planning

Issue: President and Treasurer roles have no documented succession plan. Mandated 2025/26 transitions with zero preparation creates leadership vacuum risk.

Impact: Strategic plan execution stalls; financial controls weakened; key decisions delayed across all domains.

Action: BEGIN IMMEDIATELY. Identify successor candidates, establish shadow programs, document role knowledge. Target: Succession plan published by end Q3 2026.

🔴 CRITICAL: Paid Junior Coach Not Hired

Issue: Strategic milestone is H2 2025. No recruitment started. Budget ($20-30k) not confirmed. Role not defined.

Impact: Cannot execute team expansion (3xU11, 2xU15). Volunteer coaching will break under growth demands. Talent pipeline stalls.

Action: Define job description Q4 2026. Secure budget from 2027 financial plan. Begin recruitment by Q1 2027. Hire by pre-season 2027.

🔴 CRITICAL: Turf Ground Project Undefined

Issue: Lucas Central Park is foundational. 2-3 year planning cycle overdue. Funding streams undefined. Project ownership unclear.

Impact: Cannot expand junior teams (facility constraint). Winter training unavailable. Member recruitment blocked. Sustainability goal unachievable.

Action: Establish project governance NOW. Assign project owner. Coordinate with Integra on design. Identify funding (CA infrastructure, State Gov, Council). Planning timeline: Q4 2026 - Q2 2027.

🔴 CRITICAL: Women's Senior Cricket at Risk

Issue: Women's team launched 2024/25 but lacks: women's sponsor, female coach pathway, junior girls feeder teams, sub-committee structure.

Impact: Without investment, women's program stalls after inaugural season. Junior girls pathway (U14, U17) won't materialize. Diversity goal unmet.

Action: Recruit women's cricket sponsor Q4 2026. Hire female coach for juniors by 2025/26. Establish female sub-committee with 2 committee members. Launch U14 girls 2025/26.

🔴 CRITICAL: Junior Coordinator Bottleneck

Issue: One person owns 13+ junior capabilities: program design, governance, coaching, logistics, player development, member engagement, HPA, women's program, uniforms, finance.

Impact: Cannot scale junior program from current size. No succession; if role vacant, program collapses. Burnout risk high.

Action: De-scope role immediately. Create Junior Committee with distributed responsibilities. Separate "Member Engagement" and "Finance" to dedicated people. Identify backfill for succession.

Strategic Dependencies

Executive Succession → All Domains

Leadership transitions cascade. Delayed decisions on coach hiring, facility investment, and women's program cascade across all capabilities. Succession plan drives decision-making speed.

Paid Junior Coach → Team Expansion & Talent Pipeline

Cannot grow juniors (3xU11, 2xU15 target) or develop women's pathway without paid coaching. Current volunteer model works at 60 players; breaks at 100+.

Turf Ground → All Expansion Plans

Facility constraint blocks junior team expansion, winter training, match scheduling, and senior team growth. 2-3 year planning cycle means decision required NOW.

Financial Health ($50k→$75k→$100k) → Investment Capacity

Bank balance target drives ability to fund paid coach, female coach, facility upgrades, sponsorships. Cannot invest without revenue growth. Commercial domain maturity critical.

Talent Pipeline (15+ u18-21) → Senior Team Sustainability

Strategic goal of 1st Grade readiness depends on junior program delivering graduates to senior system. Requires junior coaching quality (35%), player development (40%), and senior team capacity.
