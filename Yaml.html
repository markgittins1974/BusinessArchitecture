Crossover Points — All Three Procedures

Crossover 1: AOS Q7 exemption requests are referred into the TARB procedure for determination.
Crossover 2: TARB determinations that affect AOS registrations flow back to AOS Q8 for system updates.
Crossover 3: Matters initially classified as TARB items may be reclassified as simple AOS amendments handled by LRS.
Crossover 4: BTR concession (Q3) automatically triggers AOS exemption on eligible land — direct system dependency.
Crossover 5: BTR eligibility edge cases (ownership structure, rental terms interpretation) may require a TARB private ruling. This link is implied, not proceduralised.
