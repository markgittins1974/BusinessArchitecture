Business Architecture

This page provides a conceptual view of the business architecture, bringing together capabilities, value streams, and processes to support shared understanding and design discussions.

Business Capabilities

Business capabilities describe what the organisation must be able to do, independent of organisational structure or systems.

  • Customer Management
  • Revenue Assessment
  • Payment & Refund Management
  • Compliance & Enforcement

Value Streams

Value streams describe how value is delivered end‑to‑end to customers and stakeholders by orchestrating multiple capabilities.

  • Determine Liability
  • Collect Revenue
  • Manage Compliance
  • Resolve Customer Enquiries

Processes

Processes describe how work is executed to realise capabilities
